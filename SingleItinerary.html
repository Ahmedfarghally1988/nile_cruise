Tour Highlights :

  • Explore Aswan with a visit to the Philae Temple and the magnificent Aswan High Dam.

  • Visit the unique Temple of Kom Ombo, dedicated to two gods—Sobek, the crocodile god, and Horus, the falcon god.

  • Enjoy private stops at Bissau and Beshier Islands, perfect for peaceful walks and sunset views.

  • Wander through the mystical quarries of Gebel El Selsela, an ancient source of Egypt’s greatest monuments.

  • Sail to Edfu and discover the well-preserved Temple of Horus.

  • Witness the engineering marvel of passing through the Esna Lock.

  • Explore the stunning Temple of Karnak and the majestic Valley of the Kings in Luxor.
